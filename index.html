22 a Day

Since 2010, June was acknowledged as National PTSD Awareness Month with June 27th as PTSD Awareness Day. In 2016, the VA recently released a report that suggests that on average, as many as 22 veterans commit suicide every day.  This site serves to give credence to this number and build an intuition for the relative  severity of this situation. For a more detailed look at the numbers, simply hover over what you are  interested in looking at!
